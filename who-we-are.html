Who We Are

Magnus Tempus is a leading innovator in time and attendance solutions, specializing in modern, user-friendly systems that streamline workforce management. Founded with a vision to revolutionize how businesses track employee time, we've developed Punch Clock Pro - a cutting-edge iPad-based time clock solution that seamlessly integrates with Attendance on Demand.

Our team combines decades of industry experience with modern technical expertise to deliver solutions that are both powerful and easy to use. We understand the challenges businesses face in managing their workforce, and we're committed to providing tools that make this process simpler and more efficient.

At Magnus Tempus, we believe in continuous innovation and exceptional customer service. We work closely with our clients to ensure our solutions meet their specific needs and help drive their business forward.
